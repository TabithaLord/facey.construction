About the company

Built for residential and business clients across The Bahamas.

Facey Construction & Renovations is a Bahamian-owned subcontractor specializing in high-quality renovations, custom carpentry, and commercial buildouts.

The team combines modern craftsmanship with dependable execution to deliver durable, value-adding improvements for homes, offices, retail spaces, and commercial properties.
